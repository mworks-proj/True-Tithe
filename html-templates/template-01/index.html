Who we are

About Us

At Tithe, we believe in the power of modern technology to enhance the donations process and bridge the gap between digital payments and financial inclusion. We are dedicated to providing churches and nonprofit organizations with a streamlined and secure way to accept donations.

We are looking for developers and engineers passionate about creating modern solutions to join us in building products that make a positive impact on the world.

    • Our Mission

    Tithe's mission is to empower religious organizations with the ability to facilitate digital and secure tithes and offerings. Through our easy-to-use Tithe Template, we aim to make it accessible to any church or fundraising organization to accept payments from their members and donators without any coding skills.

    Donate Now Learn More

    Our Values

    Tithe is committed to creating innovative solutions for churches and fundraisers that are rooted in financial access and sustainability. We strive to foster an equitable financial system by engineering frictionless payment methods. Across each decision we make, we prioritize security, efficiency, and improving the overall donations process for religious institutions.
